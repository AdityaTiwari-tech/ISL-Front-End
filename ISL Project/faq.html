FAQs

↗️

What is ISL Smart Communicator?

ISL Smart Communicator is a web app that detects Indian Sign Language (ISL) gestures and translates them into text and voice in real-time, helping bridge the communication gap.

↗️

Who can use this app?

Anyone! Whether you are from the Deaf community, a student learning ISL, or someone wanting to communicate better, this app is designed for you.

↗️

Do I need to download anything?

No, ISL Smart Communicator works directly on your web browser — no downloads or installations needed.
